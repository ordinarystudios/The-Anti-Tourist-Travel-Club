Itinerary

Day 1

Arrive in Cairo and settle in at beautiful boutique hotel Villa Belle Epoque. Once a British Colonial Manor House, the hotel is situated in the affluent Maadi neighbourhood. Lounge by the pool before congregating with the group for an Egyptian feast and live music in the lush hotel gardens in the centre of Cairo.

Day 2

Discover the one thing on everyone’s travel bucket list, the Great Pyramids of Giza. Venture into the desert for the best photo angle of this epic site, a little local expertise. From there, we will explore the mummies and relics of the famous Egypt museum, including the mask of King Tut with a local tour guide.

Day 3

We will start our morning by visiting the famous Cairo citadel and some of the cities most beautiful mosques. In the evening we will fly to Luxor and check in at the incredible Winter Palace. The Winter Palace was originally the Egyptian king’s winter palace, seriously… Stroll amongst the bougainvillea in the garden and stop for a nightcap in the piano bar to imagine the conversations Howard Carter and Lord Carnarvan once had there.

Day 4

Wake up early for a guided tour of Luxor’s mystifying tombs including Valley of the Kings, Valley of the Queens & Hatshepsuts temple. We will go back in time to when Luxor was the capital of Egypt and Pharaohs ruled the land. Spend the afternoon by the pool or exploring the banks of the nile and the streets of Luxor. We will take a sunset boat ride down the Nile, the best way to truly see how people live in one of the oldest cities in the world.

Day 5

Go back in time thousands of years at Luxor & Karnak Temple. Later, make the drive as a group to Aswan and spend the evening at the Movenpick.

Day 6

Soak in the stories of gods, godesses and pharoahs at the temples of Aswan. Later, visit a Nubian village before an afternoon sail on the Nile. Take in the breathtaking sunset view while sipping a beverage from the patio of the Old Cataract.

Day 7

The day will begin by visiting one of the most breathtaking sites in Egypt, Abu Simbel. In the afternoon return to Cairo and relax at Villa Belle Epoque or explore the neighbourhood of Maadi.

Day 8

Travel to the turquoise waters of Ein El Sokhna on the Red sea for a day long Yacht Party. Private yacht, your new best friends, basically heaven on earth.

Day 9

Say our goodbyes and head home.
